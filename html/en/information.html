How will the integration unwind itself ?

At the start of the school year, three intense integration weeks will await you

Activities, games, visits and parties will be organized by CAPA members to help you discover the Douaisian lifestyle.

You will also meet second and third year students who can give you advice for the years to come.

Schedule

Here is the integration schedule. It might evolve because of the current health situation.

Your return to school begins on Thursday, September 02, 2021.

We'll be ready to welcome you as early as Saturday August 28 to start your integration.

We'll be present from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. on Saturday August 28 and Sunday August 29 at the Lavoisier residence to greet you.

Remember to come as soon as possible and ideally on Saturday August 28, this will give you time to settle in, meet new people and participate in the festivities with all the new arrivals. We will also show you around the Maison des Élèves.

(called the Meud).

Parties

Warm Up Night: this is the first night of your integration fillot ! So once your things are unpacked and your marks taken in the residence, come join us to discuss and dance with your classmates and us, the CAPA!

Terroir Evening: for this evening, bring a specialty from your region to share with others, during a giant appetizer! Sweet and savory products will be much appreciated ! If you realize that you don't have a specialty from where you come from, then just bring something that you enjoy and that you want to share with everyone!

Find your other half party: Tonight, everyone will have a bracelet with a number. And here comes the twist ! Another person will have the same number as you ! So don't waste time, and go chat with everyone of your class in order to find your other half !

Freshwater Marine Evening: the theme of this evening is the beach! You are free to dress up according to the theme: beach umbrella, swim shorts, hats and espadrilles are of the essence !

Chad evening: For this exceptional evening fillot, please bring back your best redneck outfit, with which you can impress all your friends by dancing to Johnny Halliday !

List Night: this evening celebrates all the lists created last year for the various student offices. So go talk to the 2nd years and learn more about the student offices campaigns !

Cross-dressing Evening: for this evening, the genres are reversed! Girls dress as boys and boys dress girls. So really let go of yourself with makeup, fake mustaches, dresses, everything is welcome! Who will push the game the furthest ???

Evening M like: Well you're on your own for this one, just let your imagination run wild, and disguise yourself as something that starts with M! So who will be the most original ?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find the most common questions asked about moving in and integrating the school.

We invite you to read the meudien's guide and browse the site before coming here.


How will your 3 weeks of integration present itself?


Upon your arrival, you will be assigned to a team. The objective will be to earn the most points by completing daily challenges. So prepare yourself champion and let the best team win.


Will your integration be “normal”?


Unfortunately we can not know the future, nevertheless we've anticipated the most positive as well as the most negative scenarios. In any case you will have a crazy integration! Rest assured, hell week is prohibited so you won't be bothered of bullying from older students.


How is accomodated the MEUD ?


You can get a first glimpse of the housing offered by the school in Douai by going to page 10 of the meudien guide. But the virtual tours might give you a better glimpse: just go to the Life in Douai page. You can get additional information by going to the IMT Lille-Douai website in the Student life accommodation:


https://imt-lille-douai.fr/experience-etudiante/logements/

How do you get housing at the MEUD?


To stay at the MEUD, the residences of IMT Lille Douai in Douai, you must be registered administratively at the school. This will give you your login details to connect to MyServices, the IMT Lille Douai platform. Once logged in, click on the “Housing” icon where you can pre-register. Pre-registrations will be available around mid-June.


What happens if you don't stay at the MEUD ?


Damn, we weren't able convince you... Know that your school card will still allow you to access the residences up to 9pm. You will not have access to the car garage. Nevertheless you will of course still be able to go to the school cafeteria, which is located in the Lavoisier residence.


Do you have a car and want to have access to the underground garage?


This is possible for the fee of 22 euros per month when you are a resident of the MEUD.


Douai, is it really that bad ?


Aaaaahhhh lets cut the chase. A lot of students have asked themselves that question. Well Douai is not the most dynamic city you will ever visite, we're in a provincial town where you can take pleasant stroll along the Scarpe. Nevertheless fillot do not forget that this isn't it. The school atmosphere will make you forget all the disadvantages of the North. Just remember, that in the North we only cry twice: once when we arrive and another time when we leave.


What is the workload?


Difficult to assess, as it depends on the individual. Regular work is enough to pass the exams, and no worries you will not need to work 3 hours every night. Nevertheless be careful fillot.e because your L3 grades are very important for your application to foreign universities. In addition, your ranking will give you priority (or not) for modules within the school.


L3 elective modules to recommend or not ?


No, it all depends on what you like. Do not hesitate to discuss this kind of subject with your godfather or your godmother, they are here for that.


Is the canteen good?


Don't worry, you'll be able to eat your fill ! Our chefs are always there to prepare good balanced dishes with several options. Vegetarian can also find meals specially prepared for them !
